My name is Omar Wael, and I’m a 16-year-old student from Egypt. I’ve always enjoyed learning, but what I enjoy even more is teaching others. I believe it’s unfair to know something useful and keep it to yourself, especially when sharing it could help someone grow or feel more confident. That’s why I’ve loved teaching from a young age, even when the topic is simple or basic. For me, teaching isn’t about being an expert—it’s about helping others understand, improve, and realize that learning doesn’t have to be complicated.

About the website

If you were expecting something different, feel free to message me—I’m more than happy to hear your thoughts and suggestions! Wishing you a great month :)

purpose:

My purpose is to make learning more accessible and enjoyable for everyone. try my best to help people around the world in any way I can, because I don’t think it’s fair that someone’s desire to learn is limited by their financial situation. That’s why I keep doing this. I’ll continue sharing what I know and helping others in every way I can, even if what I’m teaching feels small or simple. Knowledge has value no matter its size, and if it can help even one person move forward, then it’s worth sharing.
