So you feel like you failed?

First of all, I don’t really believe there’s such a thing as “failing.” Maybe you set a monthly goal and didn’t fully reach it—but that’s not failure. You made a plan, committed to a goal, and saw improvement, and that alone is a win, even if you hoped for more. Take a step back, look at what went wrong, and learn from it instead of beating yourself up. You can always adjust the plan or break it into smaller pieces. Never tell yourself something is “too hard.” Nothing is too hard until you try and prove it isn’t. You don’t discover your limits without pushing past what feels comfortable. Dream as big as you can, don’t be afraid of falling short, and remember—no goal is too big for you.
